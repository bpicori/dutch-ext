# TabTaal Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 20, 2026

TabTaal is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with Dutch language practice challenges.

## Data we collect

TabTaal does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. We have no servers and no user accounts.

## Data stored on your device

To track your learning progress, TabTaal stores the following locally in `chrome.storage.local` on your device only:

- Challenge progress and spaced-repetition scheduling
- Review history (which cards you answered, and whether you were correct)
- Daily review counts and streaks
- Cards you have chosen to ignore

This data never leaves your browser. The developer cannot access it.

## Third parties

TabTaal does not share data with third parties. The extension loads fonts from Google Fonts to display text; no personal data is sent as part of that request.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in this repository.
